Privacy Policy

App: Quick Tables
Developer: Vincsz
Last updated: April 15, 2025

1. Overview

Quick Tables is a multiplication tables quiz app for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is — and is not — collected when you use the app.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Quick Tables does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data is sent to any server or third party

3. Data Stored on Device

The app may store game preferences or scores locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer.

4. Children's Privacy

Quick Tables is suitable for all ages, including children. Because we collect no personal data, the app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and the GDPR provisions applicable to minors.

5. Applicable Laws

This policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – EU), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and equivalent laws in other jurisdictions. Since no personal data is collected, no data processing takes place under these regulations.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us via GitHub: github.com/Vincsz
